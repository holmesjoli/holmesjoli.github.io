<  History of Information Visualization  />

Brief

Create an information visualization that summarizes the history of information visualizations from Michael Friendly's research compilation: Milestones Tour. The Milestones tour attempts to comprehensively document all scientific and graphic advances in information visualization.

Methods

To collect the information for the visualization, I web scraped the Milestones Tours website, then organized and selected the data to visualize in Illustrator. Click here to download a PDF of this visualization.
