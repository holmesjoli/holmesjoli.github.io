About

I currently live in Houston, Texas and work at the Texas Policy Lab (TPL) at Rice University. At TPL, I work in our Health, Early Child Care, and Criminal Justice policy areas. Prior to my work at TPL, I worked at Mathematica Policy Research in their Healthcare division.

Originally, I'm from Bainbridge Island, Washington, but have lived in four states in the US and Melbourne, Australia. I identify as a member of the LGBTQ+ community and am very interested in data issues such as privacy and data collection that are particularly sensitive to this community.

I graduated from Wesleyan University with a BA in Economics and a minor in data analytics in 2017. I began programming at Wesleyan 6 years ago and have touched code almost every day since. Prior to attending Wesleyan University, I attended Whitman College for a year.

In my spare time I throw ceramics on the wheel, play ultimate frisbee, and try to get outside as much as possible. I've had a love of art my whole life.
